CellBricks

A New Cellular Architecture

CellBricks is a democratized cellular ecosystem that decouples user management from network infrastructure.

It is a dull time for the cellular ecosystem. A small number of major carriers have monopolized the market. They hold over 98% of subscribers and produce hundreds of pages of specifications that have raised the barrier to entry for new entrants. Unsatisfied with the monopolistic nature of this market, a group of researchers decide to tackle the problem of enabling a cellular ecosystem wherein competition thrives.

The CellBricks project re-examines the cellular provider landscape. Currently, small players and new entrants are challenged by the vast infrastructure costs needed to expand service. The CellBricks protocol aims to significantly reduce the barrier of entry by decoupling physical infrastructure from user management and billing.
